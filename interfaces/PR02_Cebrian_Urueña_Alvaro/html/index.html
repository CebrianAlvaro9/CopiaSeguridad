Program

Imagine a folk festival with may differents tents-tents for all diferrents sorts of music, a tent where you learn how to fiddle, and maybe one where you make drums. each has its focus. People move from tent to tent making up their owmn experience

The Drumbeat Festival will work exactly like this. we'll have have all kinds of spaces where you can teach and learn-studios, labs, palygrounds, classrooms and even few tents in the public square. Each space will provide a place to dive deep and get your hands dirty with some of the most interesting ideas at the interesection of learning, freedom and the web

Badge lab

Test, critique and improve badges and tools that recognize informal online learning. Hosted by P2PU, Mozilla and Remix learning

Webcraft toolshed

Get your hands dirty testing and improving standsards-bases web developer courses. Hosted by P2PU, Mozilla

Peer-learning fishbowl

Provide feedback on peer-to-peer courses under development and chart new courses. Hosted by Peer 2 Peer University

Local learning incubator

Help Librarians and community organizers produce new open web learning content. Hosted by

Open content studio

Hack on open textbooks and help build a global courseware catalog. Hosted Flat world, kwogledfe, OCW consortium, Creative commons and Conexions

Hackerspace playground

Learn how to make teach with processing.js arduino a maker bot and the other cool toys Hosted by Monochron and Arduino

Wikipedia lounge

Pitch in on projects that fuse Wikipedia into the world of learning and educatioan.Hosted by Wikimedia foundation and wikimedia in catalan

Open source classroom

Video + learning gallery

Make, Translate and share online videos with a bent for learning. Hosted by: Free knowledge institute and seneca college
